If someone is being trafficked for sex, there are several signs that you can easily recognize.


Physical signs include:

  • Injuries or other signs of physical abuse
  • Looking malnourished
  • Branding(tattoos) on the neck or lower back
  • Always wearing the same clothes regardless of the weather or circumstances
  • Few personal possessions


In terms of behavior, a victim might:

  • Be disoriented and not know where he or she is
  • Act fearful, anxious, depressed, submissive, tense, or nervous/paranoid
  • Avoid eye contact and appear hesitant to talk to strangers
  • Seem to have rehearsed responses in social interaction
